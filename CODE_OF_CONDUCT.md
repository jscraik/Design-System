# Code of Conduct

## Our standards

- Be respectful, constructive, and specific.
- Assume positive intent, but prioritize safety and evidence.
- Do not post private credentials, secrets, or personal data.

## Enforcement

Project maintainer (Jamie) is responsible for moderation and final decisions.
Repeated harassment, abuse, or unsafe behavior may lead to removal from participation.

## Reporting

Report conduct concerns using the support/security channels described in `SUPPORT.md` and `SECURITY.md`.
